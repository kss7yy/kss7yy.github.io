Who am I?

Engineer. Researcher. Aspiring Physician.

Hello, my name is Kunaal Sarnaik and welcome to my website! Currently, I am finishing up my senior year at the University of Virginia, majoring in biomedical engineering and pursuing minors in both computer science and data science. I am extremely fascinated with the integration of cutting edge technologies such as machine learning, pattern recognition, image classification, and artificial intelligence into the field of medicine. As such, I hope to spend my life iteratively enhancing healthcare quality, outreach, and accuracy on the global scale, attempting to improve patient outcomes along the way.

Outside of school, I work as an emergency medicine scribe at the University of Virginia Health System, have participated in numerous research endeavors and extracurricular societies, and have also pursued the implementation of a novel medical device along with a classmate. I enjoy boxing, reading historical biographies, disassembling electronics, and hiking at locations scattered across Northern Virginia, where I currently reside.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out!

Electrocardiogram

Charlottesville, VA
